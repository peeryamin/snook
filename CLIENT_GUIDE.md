# Black Racks Snooker Club — User Guide

## Login

Open the link you were given and sign in with your username and password.

---

## Daily use

### 1. Start a session
- Tap **Start Session** on Table 1 or Table 2
- Enter player name (optional phone — 10 digits after +91)
- Tap **Start Session**

### 2. While playing
- Timer and running amount update automatically
- **Pause** / **Resume** for breaks

### 3. When they finish
- Tap **Stop**
- Choose how they will pay: **Cash**, **UPI**, or **Card**
- Tap **Stop and Save Bill**

### 4. When money is actually received
- Go to **Awaiting Payment**
- Tap **Mark as Paid** for that player

> Tap **Mark as Paid** when you have actually received the money (cash/UPI/card).

### 5. View today’s earnings
- Tap **View Today’s Earnings**
- Enter your admin password
- Tap **Hide Earnings** when done

---

## Table rates

| Table   | Rate      | Minimum |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Table 1 | Rs.5/min  | Rs.100  |
| Table 2 | Rs.7/min  | Rs.150  |

Short sessions pay the minimum. Longer sessions pay per minute if that is higher.

---

## Tips

- Use **Refresh** if the screen looks stuck
- Player names autocomplete if they played earlier today
- End-of-day report emails automatically if email is configured

---

## Problems?

Contact your system administrator (the person who set this up for you).
